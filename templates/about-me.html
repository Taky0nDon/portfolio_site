About Me

My name is Mike, and I'm a hyper passionate programmer. Thanks for checking out my portfolio!

I'm 34 years old, and I've felt a powerful connection to computing ever since I was a little kid. In February 2023 I began learning python, and have devoted many hours to increasing my knowledge of the craft since then.

From online courses, to good old fashioned books, to binging on Primeagen videos and late nights tackling problems on CodeWars, I strived to get as much insight and knowledge from various sources as possible.

It's been a challenge at time, but also a source of incredible joy. I love the thrill of solving problems and bending the digital world to my will.

I found myself more capable of taking advantage of all the power a modern computer gives its user. The feeling of growth and accomplishment implementing the concepts and skills I've spent so much time learning has consistently kept me motivated through the tough valleys of understanding. The carrot of increased understanding and new victories is constantly luring me forward.

Please, take a moment to check out my projects, or connect with me if you want to nerd out together.
